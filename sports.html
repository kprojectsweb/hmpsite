Welcome To Holy Mother Sports Site

Holy Mother Sports Team

We believe that with the help of sports not only a strong disciplined and healthy generation can be build but also a society where people live in harmony. The primary goal of HMECS to help children and youth to find the success in sport they need to make them feel valued and wanted.

Vision

Our vision is to build a community of disciplined, healthy and non-communal young boys and girls with social responsibility through sports.

Mission

By providing opportunities to sharpen their sports skills by training, participating in tournaments and teaching the life skills to the young boys and girls to show their talents to the world and to make it as a career.
